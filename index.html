Eazy-Eats

Our Mission

Eazy-Eats is a new tool to link restaurants and shelters with leftover food. Say goodbye to wasting leftovers and share them with those in need. On average 1.3 billion tons of food is wasted every year. With the amount of food being thrown out we as a community can help those who can not afford a meal everyday. Eazy-Eats is dedicated to the facilitation of excess food from restaurants with extra food using taxi services like Uber, Lyft, etc. or other volunteers.
